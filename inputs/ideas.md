# Ideas

## 💼 Business
- 👟 **Sneaker Rescue:** A premium sneaker cleaning service. Advertise via WhatsApp status with before/after photos.
- 👗 **Thrift Flipper:** Buy second-hand clothes, wash and iron them, and sell them on an Instagram page with cool styling.
- 🛒 **Spaza Restock Helper:** Help local Spaza shops order stock online in bulk using your phone, saving them a trip to the wholesaler.
- 🛵 **Queue Stand-in:** Get paid to stand in long lines at clinics or SASSA offices for people who have to work.
- 🍔 **Late Night Bites:** Sell warm snacks (like vetkoek or fries) to people walking home late. Promote via a WhatsApp group.
- 🧺 **Mobile Laundry:** Collect laundry in your area, wash it at home or a laundromat, and deliver it folded the next day.
- 💇🏾‍♀️ **Braiding Booker:** A WhatsApp catalog where people can see hairstyles and book appointments with local braiders.
- 🚗 **Eco Car Wash:** A water-saving car wash service that comes to the client's house on weekends.
- 🍅 **Fresh Veggie Box:** Buy veggies in bulk from the city market and deliver mixed, affordable veggie boxes locally.
- 📱 **Airtime/Data Reseller:** Buy airtime/data in bulk and resell it to neighbors late at night when shops are closed.

## 🤝 Community
- 🏥 **Clinic Line Tracker:** A WhatsApp group where you give live updates on how long the line is at the local clinic.
- 🔍 **Lost & Found Bot:** A Facebook page or WhatsApp group dedicated purely to finding lost ID books and keys.
- 🚨 **Safety Alert Network:** Organize a fast-alert WhatsApp system for your street to warn about crime or emergencies.
- 💧 **Water/Power Update:** Be the reliable source for loadshedding and water outage updates for your specific neighborhood.
- 🛠️ **Local Fix-it Match:** Know who fixes leaking pipes or broken roofs? Connect them with neighbors who need help for a small finder's fee.
- 👶 **Trusted Babysitters:** Create a small network of trusted, verified teenagers who can watch kids when single moms work late.
- 🚶🏾‍♂️ **Walking School Bus:** Provide a service where you safely walk a group of younger kids to and from school.
- 🧹 **Street Clean Crew:** Organize weekend clean-ups. Sell the recyclable plastic, glass, and cans for profit.
- ⚽ **Weekend Tournament:** Organize local 5-a-side soccer or netball tournaments. Charge a small entry fee and get a local sponsor.
- 🌱 **Community Garden:** Turn an empty patch of dirt into a veggie garden. Sell the fresh produce to neighbors.

## 📚 Learn
- 🤖 **Math AI Buddy:** Set up a free WhatsApp bot (using ChatGPT) that explains difficult math homework to students.
- 📄 **CV Creator:** Help older folks or school leavers create beautiful, professional CVs on your phone using Canva.
- 📚 **Textbook Swap:** Run a platform (Facebook group) where students can swap or buy cheap second-hand school books.
- 🎓 **Bursary Finder:** Find scholarships and bursaries online, and help local students fill out the applications.
- 🗣️ **English/Xhosa Tutor:** Offer 30-minute language practice sessions over WhatsApp voice notes for beginners.
- 💻 **Phone Coding Club:** Teach younger kids how to build basic websites using just their smartphone browsers.
- 🎤 **Interview Prep:** Do mock job interviews via video call to help people gain confidence before the real thing.
- 📖 **Story Time App:** Record audio notes of yourself reading children's books and share them with busy parents.
- 🌍 **Local Tour Guide:** Learn the deep history of your township and offer safe, authentic walking tours to tourists.
- 🧠 **Study Group Boss:** Organize quiet, focused study sessions in a local church or hall before exams. Charge for snacks.

## 🎨 Creative
- 🎨 **Flyer Designer:** Use Canva on your phone to design professional posters for local parties, funerals, or churches.
- 🎵 **Beat Maker:** Make music beats on your phone using free apps and sell them to local upcoming rappers.
- 📸 **Product Photographer:** Take high-quality photos of local food stalls or shops with your phone to help them look professional.
- 🎬 **TikTok Editor:** Edit cool, fast-paced TikTok or Reel videos for small businesses so they can get more customers.
- 😂 **WhatsApp Stickers:** Create funny, local WhatsApp stickers with township slang and sell custom packs.
- 🎙️ **Township Podcast:** Start a podcast using voice notes, interviewing local heroes and sharing real stories.
- 👕 **Custom T-Shirts:** Take orders for custom printed shirts for birthdays or events. Outsource the printing, keep the profit.
- 💍 **Upcycled Jewelry:** Make beautiful bracelets or necklaces from wire, beads, or recycled materials and sell them.
- 📝 **Poetry/Rap Blog:** Start an Instagram page featuring short poems or rap lyrics written by local youth.
- 💅🏽 **Nail Art Pro:** Do amazing, creative nail art from your home using cheap supplies and promote on TikTok.

## 💻 Tech
- 🌐 **Digital Menu Maker:** Turn paper menus from local food stalls into QR codes that people can scan with their phones.
- 📱 **Social Media Manager:** Manage the Facebook and Instagram pages for 3 local businesses. Charge them a small weekly fee.
- 📍 **Wi-Fi Map Maker:** Create a digital list or map of all the spots with free or cheap Wi-Fi in the area.
- 🧾 **Digital Receipts:** Help local plumbers or builders send professional digital invoices using free apps instead of paper.
- 📊 **Online Survey Filler:** Sign up for platforms where you get paid to fill out surveys and test new apps on your phone.
- 🔗 **Link-in-Bio Setup:** Build simple "Linktree"-style pages for local influencers or businesses using free tools.
- 🤖 **Auto-Reply Bot:** Set up WhatsApp Business auto-replies for busy plumbers or hair salons so they don't miss clients.
- 🛒 **Glide App Shop:** Use GlideApp to turn a Google Sheet into a real shopping app for a local grocery store.
- 🎟️ **Digital Tickets:** Use a simple online form to sell digital tickets for local parties, avoiding fake paper tickets.
- 📈 **Google Maps Lister:** Get paid to add local, hidden businesses to Google Maps so customers outside the area can find them.
